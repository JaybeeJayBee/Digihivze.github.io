Recipe FAQ

What makes Digihivze recipes special?

Our recipes are digitally enhanced with:

  • Step-by-step video tutorials
  • Interactive ingredient substitutions
  • Chef's notes and pro tips

Each recipe is tested by our culinary team and home cooks before release.

How do I access purchased recipes?

After your purchase is complete (whether through Shopify or Gumroad), you'll receive a confirmation email with all the necessary files and links. This includes:

  • Instant PDF download with all recipes
  • Access to our digital recipe portal
  • Lifetime updates to the collection
Can I print the recipes?

Yes! All recipes include:

  • Printer-friendly versions
  • Mobile-optimized formats
  • Scaling options for different servings
Why do you use both Shopify and Gumroad for payments?

We use both platforms to provide you with the best purchasing experience:

  • Shopify offers a robust, dedicated e-commerce experience with many payment options.
  • Gumroad provides secure, instant digital delivery, preferred by some of our users.
  • Both ensure secure payment processing and instant digital delivery of your recipes.
Are the recipes suitable for special diets?

Our collections include:

  • Clear dietary labels (vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Alternative ingredient suggestions
  • Nutritional information where applicable
How often do you release new recipes?
  • Seasonal collections: Released quarterly
  • Specialty packs: Throughout the year
  • Free updates: For existing customers
What if I'm not satisfied with a recipe collection?

We offer:

✅ 14-day refunds if you haven't downloaded the recipes

✅ Cooking support (email support)

❌ No refunds after recipe download

Can I request specific types of recipes?

Absolutely! We welcome recipe requests and suggestions. Email us at connect@digihivze.kitchen with your ideas, and we'll consider them for future collections.
